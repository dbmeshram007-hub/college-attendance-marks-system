How to add the 2026 Students (Sem-1, D2D, Transfers)
Because we built your app to be driven by Excel, updating student 
data is incredibly easy and requires zero coding.
Whenever new admissions come in, just follow these steps:
Open your Excel File: Open College_System_Seed_Data.xlsx.
Go to the Students sheet.
Add the new students to the bottom of the list:
B. Pharm Sem-1: Set Program to B. Pharm and Semester to 1.
M. Pharm Sem-1 (Pharmaceutics): Set Program to M. Pharm, Specialization to Pharmaceutics, and Semester to 1.
M. Pharm Sem-1 (PQA): Set Program to M. Pharm, Specialization to PQA, and Semester to 1.
D2D / Transfer Students: Set their Program to B. Pharm, but set their Semester to 3 (since Diploma to Degree students start in the 2nd year).
